# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Nepali individuals and families seeking legal representation or counsel — primarily local clients in Nepal. They arrive with varying legal literacy; the site must communicate competence and trustworthiness immediately, in both English and Nepali.

## Product Purpose

Law Associates (LawEvolvion) is a Nepali law firm's marketing site. It exists to convert visitors into consultations by demonstrating professional authority, approachability, and track record. Success = a client picks up the phone or submits a consultation request.

## Brand Personality

Authoritative, Trustworthy, Human — a firm that is serious about the law but genuinely cares about the person across the table.

## Anti-references

- **Generic SaaS-legal** (Clio, Lawmatics, Filevine): clean but cold, dashboard-flavored, no personality, feels like software not a firm.
- **Cream/beige AI warmth**: warm neutrals, soft gradients, "approachable" clichés — looks like every other AI-generated landing page.

## Design Principles

1. **Authority without intimidation** — the visual weight of the firm should feel grounded and serious, not stiff or bureaucratic.
2. **Typography earns trust** — serif-led hierarchy with strong weight contrast is the primary brand signal, not decorative elements.
3. **Gold is a punctuation mark** — use the gold accent to direct attention, not to decorate. One moment of gold per visual unit.
4. **Human in the details** — real photography, real names, real results. The design frames humanity, not performance.
5. **Nepali-first legibility** — both languages must read with equal comfort; never sacrifice line-length or font rendering for visual novelty.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum. Body text must hit ≥4.5:1 contrast.
- Reduced-motion alternative for all reveal animations.
- Bilingual (English + Nepali) — all UI copy must work in both languages without layout breakage.
